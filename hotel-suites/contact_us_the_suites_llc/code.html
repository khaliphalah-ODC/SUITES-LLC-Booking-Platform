Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a private viewing?

expand_more

Private viewings can be scheduled via our concierge team or through the 'Book Now' interface. We offer both in-person and virtual 4K immersive tours for our international clients.

What amenities are included in the 'Grand Suite'?

expand_more

The Grand Suite features a 24-hour dedicated butler, a private temperature-controlled wine cellar, in-suite spa facilities, and priority access to our Michelin-starred dining outlets.

Are there long-term residence options?

expand_more

Yes, The SUITES offers a limited number of long-term residential leases ranging from six months to two years, featuring all the perks of our high-end hospitality services.

What is your cancellation policy?

expand_more

Cancellations must be made at least 72 hours prior to arrival for a full refund. For specialty experiences and group bookings, a 7-day notice is required.
