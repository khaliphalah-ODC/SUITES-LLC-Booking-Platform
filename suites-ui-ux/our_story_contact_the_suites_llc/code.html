Defining the New Standard of Liberian Hospitality.

Founded with a vision to marry international luxury standards with the vibrant soul of Monrovia, THE SUITES LLC stands as a testament to Liberia's burgeoning spirit. We believe that true luxury is found in the "luxury of space"—not just physical dimensions, but the mental clarity provided by thoughtful design.

Nestled in the diplomatic heart of Sinkor, our property offers more than just a place to stay. It is a curated experience where every thread of linen, every seasonal ingredient, and every interaction is intentional. Our suites are sanctuaries designed for the global traveler who seeks quietude over clamor.

"We don't just host guests; we steward the memories of their time in our beautiful capital."
