# The SUITES LLC - Project Overview

## Brand Identity
**Name:** The SUITES LLC
**Location:** Liberia
**Style:** Emerald & Gold Editorial, Modern Luxury
**Tone:** Calm, Elegant, Premium

## Core Flows & Screens

### 1. Public Facing (Guest Acquisition)
- **Home**: Luxury hero with booking search (Check-in, Check-out, Guests, Suite Type).
- **Suites Listing**: Grid of suites with cards showing image, name, price, capacity.
- **Suite Details**: Immersive view with high-res imagery, amenities, and availability.
- **Booking Flow**: Guest info, stay details, price summary, and payment.
- **Confirmation**: Post-booking success screen.
- **Amenities & Experiences**: Showcasing local Liberian luxury and concierge services.
- **Gallery**: High-fidelity visual showcase.
- **About & Contact**: Brand story and luxury support.
- **Auth**: Login & Register.

### 2. Guest Portal (Stay Management)
- **Dashboard**: Overview of upcoming stays and loyalty points.
- **Reservations**: Detailed list of past and future bookings.
- **Payments**: Transaction history and saved methods.
- **Notifications & Profile**: Personal preferences and alerts.

### 3. Admin / Staff Dashboard (Operations)
- **Overview**: Real-time stats (occupancy, revenue, upcoming arrivals).
- **Bookings Management**: Table with filters and status badges (Confirmed, Pending, Checked-in).
- **Inventory Control**: Suites, Amenities, and Experiences management.
- **User & Message Center**: Handling guest inquiries and staff access.
- **Settings**: System configurations.
